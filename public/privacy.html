Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Welcome to Blueprint Compiler. This Privacy Policy outlines how I collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Blueprint Compiler web application ("Service").

Please note: This Service is currently operated by an individual creator, not a registered corporate entity. By using the Service, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

The Service is designed to minimize data collection. We collect the following types of information:

  • Local Storage Data: We store your project configuration, wizard state, and generated blueprint files in your browser's local storage to provide a continuous experience. You can clear this at any time by resetting your browser data or clicking "Reset" in the app.
  • API Keys (Locally Stored): When you provide API keys (e.g., Anthropic, OpenAI), they are stored locally in your browser session (`sessionStorage`) and are never sent to our servers. They are only sent directly to the respective AI provider's API.
  • Account Information (If Logged In): If you choose to create an account (via Supabase auth), we store your email address and authentication tokens to enable cross-device syncing of your blueprint library.
  • Telemetry Data: We may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., which domain or IDE was selected) to improve the Service. This data does not contain personally identifiable information or your prompt contents.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • Allow you to save, load, and manage your generated AI Blueprints across devices (if you create an account).
  • Monitor general usage patterns to fix bugs and enhance features.

3. Third-Party Services and AI Providers

We use third-party services that may access certain data:

  • AI Providers (e.g., Anthropic Claude): Your prompts, configuration data, and API queries are sent directly to the AI provider you select in order to generate the blueprints. Please review their respective Privacy Policies to understand how they handle your data.
  • Supabase: Used for authentication and database storage if you choose to save your blueprints to the cloud.
  • Stripe: If you upgrade to a paid tier, payment processing is handled securely by Stripe. We do not store your full credit card details.

4. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you have certain data protection rights, including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Since most data is stored locally in your browser, you hold the primary control over it.

To request deletion of your account (if created) or associated cloud data, please contact me at: privacy@blueprintcompiler.com.

5. Data Security

While I strive to protect your personal information using commercially acceptable means (like secure Supabase infrastructure and HTTPS), remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact me at: hello@blueprintcompiler.com.
